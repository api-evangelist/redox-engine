Most healthcare organizations have solved the data movement problem. The harder challenge (and the one AI actually requires) is data readiness. In this post, Redox CPO Rachel Witalec breaks down the three jobs of AI-ready healthcare data infrastructure and what it takes to build a composability layer that lets AI work reliably at scale.
