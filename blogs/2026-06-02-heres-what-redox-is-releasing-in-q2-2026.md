Redox is launching three major feature releases across Q2 2026 focused on improving integration management and operational visibility. The June release includes an MCP server for AI-powered integration management through tools like Claude and Cursor, plus an AI Assistant Suite offering plain-language summaries of transformations, customizations, errors, and recommended next steps. May's Workflow Viewer transforms complex logs into interactive flow diagrams for easier troubleshooting.
